# templates/ — Reusable Document Templates

These files are NOT published to the site. They are used by the
TKB Pipeline Orchestrator (Apps Script) to generate new documents
when coachees are added or sessions are recorded.

Placeholders use {curly_brace} syntax and are replaced at generation time.

## Available Templates
- coaching-profile.md — Generated when a new coachee joins
- session-note.md — Generated for each coaching session
