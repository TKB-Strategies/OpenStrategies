# The Steward's Manual

> A guide for mission-driven leaders on sustainable organizational stewardship.

---

Leadership in mission-driven organizations is an act of stewardship — holding something in trust for the communities, teams, and futures that depend on it. The Steward's Manual is a practical companion for leaders navigating the real tensions of that role: growing impact without growing burnout, maintaining accountability without losing humanity, and building systems that outlast any single leader.
