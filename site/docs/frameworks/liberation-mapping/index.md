# Liberation Mapping

> A strategic planning methodology that centers dignity, collective care, and systemic transformation.

---

Traditional strategic planning often reinforces the systems it claims to improve — centering funder priorities over community voice, optimizing for metrics that miss the point, building plans that burn out the people responsible for executing them.

Liberation Mapping inverts these defaults. It's a planning methodology designed for organizations that want their strategy to reflect their values — not just in the vision statement, but in the daily experience of everyone the organization touches.
