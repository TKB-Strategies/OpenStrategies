# Workshop Templates

> Reusable facilitation structures for training, retreats, and conference sessions.

---

These templates provide the structure — not the client-specific content. They are designed to be adapted to your organization's context, whether you're facilitating a half-day team retreat or a multi-session leadership development program.

All templates are drawn from TKB Strategies' consulting practice across nonprofits, human service organizations, and mission-driven enterprises.

*Templates are in development. Check the [project roadmap](/docs/project/roadmap) for timeline.*
